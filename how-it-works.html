HOW IT WORKS

From first call to deployment - clear, fast, and measurable.

We run a structured process that keeps you informed, reduces risk, and ships automation with confidence.

Typical timeline

  • Week 1: Discovery + mapping
  • Week 2-3: Build + testing
  • Week 4: Launch + training

Client Call & Discovery

We start with a 30-45 minute call to understand your goals, current bottlenecks, and success metrics. We identify the systems involved, stakeholders, and any compliance constraints so the project starts with clear alignment.

Outcome: a short discovery brief outlining objectives, KPIs, and the initial automation scope.

Workflow Mapping

We map the exact steps of your process - from trigger to handoff - highlighting manual steps, delays, and data gaps. This includes a decision tree for edge cases to ensure the automation behaves correctly in real situations.

Outcome: visual workflow map + prioritized automation candidates.

Build & Integration

We implement the automation, connect tools via APIs or webhooks, and add the logic that drives routing, alerts, and updates. If needed, we build lightweight scripts or middleware to bridge gaps between systems.

Outcome: working automation in a staging or sandbox environment.

QA & Security Review

We test end-to-end flows with real data samples, verify permissions, and ensure data integrity across systems. We also add monitoring and alerting for failures or unusual behavior.

Outcome: validated automation with QA checklist and security review notes.

Deployment

We deploy in phases, starting with low-risk flows, and then expand once performance is confirmed. We monitor KPIs closely and make adjustments during the rollout window.

Outcome: production deployment with live monitoring and reporting.

Training & Optimization

We train your team, document processes, and establish ownership for ongoing operations. After launch, we review performance and tune triggers, thresholds, and automations based on results.

Outcome: fully adopted automation with a roadmap for improvements.
